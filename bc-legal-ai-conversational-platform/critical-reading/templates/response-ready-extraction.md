# Response-Ready Extraction — Blank Template

For documents you're going to respond to (factums, opposing submissions,
affidavits requiring reply, papers you're critiquing).

```
DOCUMENT BEING RESPONDED TO: [title / citation]
YOUR RESPONSE VEHICLE:       [reply factum / response affidavit / rebuttal / etc.]
DEADLINE:                    [YYYY-MM-DD]


POINTS YOU MUST ADDRESS
───────────────────────
Points that would prejudice you if unaddressed:

  1. Point:      [what the document argues]
     Location:   [§ N]
     Priority:   [Critical / High / Medium]
     Your response: [how you'll respond]
     Evidence needed: [what you need to make the response]

  2. Point:      [what]
     Location:   [§ N]
     Priority:   [level]
     Your response: [how]
     Evidence needed: [what]

  3. [continue as needed]


POINTS YOU COULD ADDRESS
────────────────────────
Points that would strengthen your response but are optional:

  1. Point:      [what]
     Location:   [§ N]
     Why optional: [why not strictly needed]
     Cost of addressing: [time / attention / risk]
     Benefit of addressing: [strengthens your position how?]


POINTS YOU SHOULD NOT ADDRESS
─────────────────────────────
Points that would HURT your position if you engaged them:

  1. Point:      [what]
     Location:   [§ N]
     Why not:    [raising it dignifies a weak claim / opens a can of worms /
                  distracts from your stronger points / other]


WEAKNESSES TO EXPLOIT
─────────────────────
Weaknesses in the opposing document you can use:

  1. Weakness:   [what]
     Location:   [§ N]
     Type:       [factual / legal / logical]
     How to use: [in what part of your response, and how]


ADMISSIONS TO PRESERVE
──────────────────────
Statements in the opposing document that help you:

  1. Statement:  [what they admitted or conceded]
     Location:   [§ N]
     Why useful: [how it supports your position]


CROSS-EXAMINATION MATERIAL (if applicable)
──────────────────────────────────────────
Statements you'll want to cross on:

  1. Statement:  [what]
     Location:   [§ N]
     Cross question: [what you'll ask]
     Purpose: [impeach / clarify / lock in / expand]


AUTHORITIES CITED — VERIFICATION STATUS
───────────────────────────────────────
Every authority they cite that's load-bearing for their argument:

  1. Authority:  [citation]
     Cited for:  [proposition]
     Verified?:  [Y / N — does it actually say that?]
     Weakness?:  [if verification failed, this is attack surface]


RESPONSE OUTLINE
────────────────
Once the above is complete, sketch the response:

  ¶ 1  [opening — what you're responding to]
  ¶ 2  [your position, restated cleanly]
  ¶ 3  [first must-address point]
  ¶ 4  [second must-address point]
  ...
  ¶ N  [weaknesses in opposing]
  ¶ N+1 [admissions to preserve]
  ¶ N+2 [conclusion]

  Length target: [pages]
  Filing deadline: [date]
```
