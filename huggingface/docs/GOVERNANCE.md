# Hugging Face Governance

## Controlling rule

Hugging Face public assets are for demonstration, documentation, skills, and synthetic/reference materials only.

No real client, privileged, confidential, or live litigation material may be uploaded to public Hugging Face repositories.

## Public Space

The public Space must remain:

- static or safe demo only;
- no confidential uploads;
- no persistent matters;
- no model inference on real matter text;
- no court-ready output.

## Dataset publication

Before upload, confirm:

- no real names, file numbers, addresses, phone numbers, emails, or private exhibits;
- no copyrighted bulk corpus unless terms allow;
- source URLs and verification dates are present where legal materials are referenced;
- dataset card states legal-information-only boundary.

## Model publication

Before upload, confirm:

- no confidential training data;
- licence review complete;
- model card complete;
- evaluation results published;
- known limitations disclosed;
- outputs require verified retrieval.

## Private assets

Private HF repos are not a substitute for legal-data controls. They still require:

- authentication;
- matter ACLs;
- encryption;
- audit logs;
- privilege gates;
- retention and deletion policies.

## Secrets

Never commit:

- `HF_TOKEN`;
- model-provider keys;
- production backend URLs with credentials;
- signing keys;
- storage credentials.
