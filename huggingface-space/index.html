BC Legal AI Associate

Integrated, human-supervised legal practice and litigation support for British Columbia — research, evidence, drafting, calendaring, and matter organization.

Not a lawyer. Not legal advice. No solicitor–client relationship is created. The system proposes, organizes, checks, and drafts; qualified humans retain professional judgment. Do not put confidential client files on this public Space.

Product outcome

A supervised legal-work platform that reduces repetitive administrative work, strengthens source verification, improves record organization, and supports efficiency and accountability — not autonomous legal practice.
