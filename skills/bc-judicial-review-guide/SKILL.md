# BC Judicial Review Guide (Scaffold)

## Purpose

Guide JR analysis and drafting for the **Supreme Court of British Columbia**.

## Always verify before filing

Limitation periods, Rules of Court forms, service, and current *Judicial Review Procedure Act* / *Administrative Tribunals Act* provisions change. Confirm on BC Laws / CanLII / Court Rules before filing.

## Core analysis modules

1. **Amenability** — Is the decision subject to JR?  
2. **Standing** — Who may bring the petition?  
3. **Timing** — Limitation / delay / extension.  
4. **Exhaustion / adequate alternative remedies**  
5. **Standard of review** — Reasonableness / correctness (and statutory variants).  
6. **Procedural fairness** — Baker factors / context.  
7. **Record** — What is the tribunal record; affidavit limits.  
8. **Remedies** — Quash, remit, declare, stay, mandamus-type relief as available.  
9. **Costs**
