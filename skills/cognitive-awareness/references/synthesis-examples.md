# Cross-Domain Synthesis Examples

## Tenancy + Charter / human rights
Primary: RTA/RTB. Adjacent: BCHRT Code s. 10.  
Seam: eviction after disability disclosure → retaliation + discrimination dual-file.

## IntentKernel + legal standing
Analogy: capability tokens ↔ authority to act on a resource.  
Use when explaining zero ambient authority; do not force into court pleadings.

## Procedure + substance
Evidence pack (procedure) without RTA sections (substance) is incomplete.  
Hard handoff both ways.

## Bad analogies (avoid)
- “Court is just like a chat UI” (underplays stakes/rules)
- “Always settle = always fold” (ignores leverage map)
