# Situational Signals Vocabulary

## Urgency
today · asap · hearing Monday · deadline · before EOD · order of possession

## Stakes
$ amounts · eviction · criminal · health · production outage · “self-rep”

## Expertise high
statute cites · IRAC · “standard of review” · “privative clause” · stack traces

## Expertise low
“what’s a petition” · “explain like I’m new” · heavy acronym asks

## Format frustration
just give me · no bullets · TL;DR · shorter · stop asking questions

## Ambiguity
kind of · something like · maybe · sort of · I think we need

## Emotion
exhausted · panicked · “they’re lying” · “unfair” · all caps

## Response modes (quick)

| Combo | Mode |
|-------|------|
| Urgent + legal stakes | Direct structure + VERIFY + counsel flag |
| Ambiguous + high stakes | Clarify first |
| Learning request | learning-mode |
| Doc product | doc-coauthoring offer |
