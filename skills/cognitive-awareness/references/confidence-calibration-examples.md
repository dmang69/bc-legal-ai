# Confidence Calibration Examples

| Claim | Bucket | Risk | Language |
|-------|--------|------|----------|
| “RTA s. 6 prohibits contracting out” (after BC Laws check) | Known | High | “The Act provides that…” + cite |
| “Dispute window for Ten Day is often 5 days” | Believed | Critical | Hedge + VERIFY, or look up |
| “No permits suggests renoviction risk” | Inferred | Medium | “Based on X, this suggests…” |
| “Arbitrator will award 30% abatement” | Guessed | High | “My best guess… depends on evidence” |
| Exact form number without RTB site | Unknown | Critical | “I’d need to check the current form” |

## Anti-confabulation

“I think security deposits are half a month” without check → Believed/Guessed, not Known. For legal deadlines, upgrade or refuse to state as fact.
