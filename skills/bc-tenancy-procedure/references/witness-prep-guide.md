# Witness Prep Guide (RTB)

> Legal information only.

## Interview flow

1. Narrative (open)  
2. Dates and documents (narrow)  
3. Confirm written summary  
4. Sort direct vs hearsay  

## Testimony rules (give to witness)

1. Answer only what is asked.  
2. “I don’t recall” > guessing.  
3. Personal knowledge only.  
4. Stay calm.  
5. Pause is OK.
