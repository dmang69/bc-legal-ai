## Mission

Expand precise legal vocabulary and transferable skills continuously — with **authoritative sources**, not invented definitions.

## Source hierarchy

1. BC Laws / official statutes (binding text)  
2. Black’s Law Dictionary / Oxford Dictionary of Law (standard definitions — quote only if verified)  
3. Cases (judicial construction)  
4. Secondary commentary (background only)  

## On each new term

Build a definition card → add to `lexicon/glossary.md` → verify pins → patch domain skills if recurring.
