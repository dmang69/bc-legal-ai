# CanLII Book of Authorities Builder

## Absolute rule

**Never invent cases, neutral citations, or pinpoints.**  
If not verified: `[CITATION UNVERIFIED — confirm on CanLII before filing]`.

## Workflow

1. Collect candidate authorities from counsel list / research.  
2. For each: locate on CanLII; record full style of cause, neutral citation, court, year.  
3. Extract principle + pinpoint paragraphs.  
4. Map each authority to an issue in the brief.  
5. Build BOA index: tab number, short title, pinpoints used.  
6. Distinguish adverse authorities in a separate section.  

## BOA table template

| Tab | Style of Cause | Neutral Citation | Court | Principle | Pinpoint | Issue # |
|-----|----------------|------------------|-------|-----------|----------|---------|
| 1 | … | … | … | … | para. _ | 1 |

## Output sections

1. Index of authorities  
2. Statutes / regulations  
3. Cases (binding first, then persuasive)  
4. Secondary materials (if any)  
5. Unverified queue (must clear before filing)
