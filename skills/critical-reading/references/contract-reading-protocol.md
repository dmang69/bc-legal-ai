# Contract Reading Protocol

## First pass

- Parties, date, term, governing law  
- Definitions section  
- Payment / performance / termination / liability / dispute  

## Second pass

```
OBLIGATION | WHO | WHEN | CONDITION | REMEDY
```

## Red flags

- Undefined key terms used normatively  
- Conflict between schedules and body  
- Automatic renewals / accelerations  
- Indemnities broader than foreseeable risk  
- Entire agreement + silent side promises  

## BC tenancy note

RTA **s. 5** (cannot contract out) may void inconsistent terms — verify on BC Laws; contract reading does not override statute.
