# Academic Paper Reading Protocol

## First pass

- Abstract + conclusion only → stated contribution  
- Figures/tables → empirical claim shape  
- Skip related-work deep-dive until contribution clear  

## Second pass

```
CONTRIBUTION (1–2 sentences):
METHOD:
EVIDENCE / RESULTS:
LIMITATIONS (stated):
LIMITATIONS (unstated / hidden assumptions):
WHAT YOU CAN CITE SAFELY:
```

## Discipline

Most of the paper is **context**. Don’t confuse literature survey with contribution.  
Preprints: mark peer-review status in confidence calibration (Believed until checked).
