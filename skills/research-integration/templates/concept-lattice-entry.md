## Maintenance

Add entries as concepts appear in more than one work. Review the whole
lattice every 6 months and:

- Merge entries that turn out to be the same concept
- Split entries that turn out to be two concepts
- Retire entries where the concept was a phase and is no longer central
- Promote entries where the concept has become signature
