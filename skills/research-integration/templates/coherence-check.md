## What Coherence Actually Means

Coherence does NOT mean every work is on the same narrow topic. It means
the works share a recognizable style, method, or set of concerns that
would let a reader familiar with your portfolio identify a new work of
yours by its shape.

If your portfolio has that recognizability, it's coherent — even if it
spans physics, computing, and policy.

If it doesn't, the coherence check is the diagnostic. Fix it, or
acknowledge that you're running two research programs in parallel.
